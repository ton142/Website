My Journey

Duration: 7 Years and Ongoing

Role: Content Creator, Gaming and Montage Editing, Streaming

Tools: Sony Vegas, Canva, StreamLabs/OBS, Elgato Game Capture

Welcome to my Youtube, Gaming and Streaming Page. I've been a part of Youtube for a long time as it is one of my passions to play video games, edit content into montage clips and interact with the community through streaming. Playing video games and following Esports is one of my favorite hobbies.

YouTube is a platform for me to release my montages and content creation. I started out as a Call Of Duty Gameplay-Commentary creator, where I showcased high quality gameplay, tips/tricks and entertainment. During this time, I often created Montages to showcase the skill and plays only the best players can execute. It allows me to be creative in telling a story through video clips and music.

Content creating, editing and interacting with the community has given me opportunities to continue showcasing high quality gameplay, interact with followers, and to create enjoyable content. I have streamed on Twitch as a way to further connect with viewers and play the games I love. Most games I stream are Teamfight Tactics, League of Legends, and OSU!

I mostly play games in the genres of FPS, RTS 3rd Person Shooters, MOBA and AutoChess genres.

Overview

I currently post YouTube videos and Stream occassionaly on Twitch. The type of Content I post is Gaming videos related to Teamfight Tactics to share my strategies, and League of Legends - where I play with my friends. I may consider branching out, however Gaming is my main interest.

I use Sony Vegas and Vegas Aftereffects to create my montages, where I add special effects, transitions and other add-ons to make videos visually entertaining and pleasing. I create my own custom Thumbnails using Canva and refer to outside sources for my image overlays. I do my best to follow the YouTube Copyright Rules while using music either in the background or in montages.

I Stream occassionally on Twitch, however I do not have a set schedule. It is a little inconsistent, however I enjoy reaching out and interacting with those who stop by and enjoy the content I produce. I stream using StreamLabs and use a headset with a built in microphone to interact with viewers.

Projects and Collaborations

Being a part of the YouTube Community has allowed me to interact and collaborate with other aspiring content creators within the same genre as me. I have created and released montages for other people's channels as well as showcase quick edits for those who like my work. Creating and growing with others is something that is fun and enjoyable.

Ranging from Quick Edits to More Gaming Montages, it is all a good experience to practice editing and providing content for others to share and grow a fanbase!
